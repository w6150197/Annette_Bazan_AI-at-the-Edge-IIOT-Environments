This module presents a case study analysis of autonomous agents in Industry 4.0, focusing on how intelligent systems can operate with minimal human intervention in industrial environments. The assignment examines the implementation of autonomous agents using reinforcement learning, as well as their role in improving decision-making, automation, and efficiency in smart manufacturing systems. The analysis also evaluates the benefits achieved, challenges encountered, and future implications of deploying autonomous agents in real-world industrial settings. In addition, a short reflection explores how this technology impacts the understanding of AI-driven automation in modern Industry 4.0 environments.
