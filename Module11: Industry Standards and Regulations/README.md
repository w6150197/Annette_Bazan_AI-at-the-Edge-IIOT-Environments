This module focuses on industry standards, governance frameworks, and regulatory policies related to AI, Edge Computing, and Industrial IoT systems. It explores how governments and international organizations are developing rules to ensure the safe, ethical, and responsible use of artificial intelligence technologies across different regions. The module examines current AI regulations in the US, EU, and Asia-Pacific, as well as global compliance frameworks that impact how IoT and edge systems are designed and deployed. It also discusses the balance between innovation and regulation, highlighting how standards influence system security, interoperability, and responsible AI development in real-world applications.
